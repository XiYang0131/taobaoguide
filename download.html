Download Taobao App

Get started with Taobao on your device

iOS Devices

Download Taobao on your iPhone or iPad

1

Change App Store Region

Go to Settings > [Your Name] > Media & Purchases > Country/Region > Change to China

2

Search for Taobao

Open App Store and search for "淘宝" or "Taobao"

3

Download and Install

Tap "Get" to download and install the app

Android Devices

Download Taobao on your Android phone or tablet

1

Enable Unknown Sources

Go to Settings > Security > Enable "Unknown Sources"

2

Download APK

Download the Taobao APK file from our secure link

3

Install the App

Open the downloaded APK file and tap "Install"

Troubleshooting

Can't find Taobao in App Store?

Make sure you've changed your App Store region to China. Taobao is primarily available in the Chinese App Store.

APK won't install?

Ensure you've enabled installation from unknown sources in your security settings.

App crashes after installation?

Try restarting your device. If the problem persists, uninstall and reinstall the app.
