Understanding the Taobao Interface

Home Screen Overview

When you first open Taobao, you'll see the home screen with various sections. At the top is the search bar, followed by promotional banners, category shortcuts, and recommended products.

Search Bar

Located at the top of the screen, use this to search for products by keywords.

Navigation Bar

At the bottom of the screen, you'll find navigation icons for Home, Categories, Messages, Cart, and My Taobao.

Taobao home screen with key areas highlighted

Searching for Products

Search Basics

Taobao's search function is powerful but can be overwhelming for new users. Here's how to effectively search for products:

1

Enter Keywords

Type relevant keywords in the search bar at the top of the screen. You can search in English, but Chinese keywords often yield better results.

2

Apply Filters

After searching, use filters to narrow down results by price range, shipping options, seller ratings, etc.

3

Sort Results

Sort results by price, popularity, sales volume, or newest listings to find what you're looking for faster.

Advanced Search Techniques

To find exactly what you're looking for, try these advanced search techniques:

  • Use specific terms rather than general ones (e.g., "cotton summer dress" instead of just "dress")
  • Include sizes, colors, or materials in your search terms
  • Use the image search feature to find similar products
  • Check the "Ships to" filter to ensure the item can be shipped to your country

Taobao search results page with filters highlighted

Pro Tip

Try using Taobao's image search feature by tapping the camera icon in the search bar. You can upload a picture of an item you like, and Taobao will find similar products.

Understanding Product Pages

Product Page Overview

Product pages on Taobao contain a wealth of information. Here's what to look for:

Product Images

Swipe through multiple product images. Tap to enlarge for a closer look.

Price Information

The current price is displayed prominently, often with original price for comparison.

Seller Information

Check the seller's rating, positive feedback percentage, and how long they've been on Taobao.

Shipping Details

Information about shipping costs, methods, and estimated delivery times.

Customer Reviews

Read reviews from other buyers, often with photos of the actual received items.

Selecting Product Options

Many products on Taobao come with various options such as size, color, or style. To select your preferred options:

  1. Tap on the "Select" button or the product image
  2. A popup will appear showing available options
  3. Select your preferred options (size, color, etc.)
  4. Adjust quantity if needed
  5. Tap "Add to Cart" or "Buy Now"

Taobao product page with key sections highlighted

Important Note

Always check the size charts carefully when buying clothing or shoes. Chinese sizes often run smaller than Western sizes. Look for measurement details in centimeters for the most accurate sizing.

Using the Shopping Cart

Shopping Cart Overview

The shopping cart allows you to collect items from different sellers before proceeding to checkout. Here's how to use it effectively:

1

Adding Items

On a product page, select your options and tap "Add to Cart" to add an item without immediately checking out.

2

Accessing Your Cart

Tap the shopping cart icon in the bottom navigation bar to view all items in your cart.

3

Managing Items

In your cart, you can adjust quantities, remove items, or save them for later. Items are grouped by seller.

4

Selecting Items for Checkout

Check the boxes next to items you want to purchase, then tap "Submit Order" to proceed to checkout.

Taobao shopping cart interface

Pro Tip

Grouping items by seller can help reduce shipping costs. Consider purchasing multiple items from the same seller when possible.

Checkout Process

Checkout Overview

The checkout process on Taobao involves several steps. Here's a walkthrough:

1

Shipping Address

Select or add a shipping address. For international shipping, you may need to use a forwarding service.

2

Shipping Method

Choose your preferred shipping method for each seller. Options and costs vary by seller and destination.

3

Leave a Message (Optional)

You can leave a message for the seller with special requests or questions.

4

Review Order

Review your order details, including items, quantities, shipping costs, and total amount.

5

Payment

Select your payment method and complete the payment to finalize your order.

Taobao checkout page

Important Note

For international shipping, you may need to use a forwarding service like Superbuy, Wegobuy, or Basetao. These services provide a Chinese address for your purchases and then forward them to your international address.

Payment Methods

Available Payment Options

Taobao offers several payment methods, though some may be limited for international users:

Alipay

Taobao's preferred payment method. International users can link certain credit cards to Alipay.

Credit/Debit Cards

Some international cards are accepted, particularly Visa and Mastercard.

Chinese Bank Transfer

Only available for users with Chinese bank accounts.

Payment Agents

Third-party services that can make payments on your behalf for a fee.

Payment Security

Taobao uses an escrow payment system to protect buyers:

  1. When you pay, the money is held by Alipay (not immediately given to the seller)
  2. The seller ships your order after receiving payment confirmation
  3. You confirm receipt of the items in good condition
  4. Only then is the payment released to the seller

Taobao payment interface

Pro Tip

If you're using a forwarding service, you'll typically pay them directly using international payment methods, and they'll handle the payment to Taobao sellers.

Shipping Options

Domestic Shipping in China

If you're in China, Taobao offers several domestic shipping options:

  • Express Delivery (快递) - Fast delivery, usually 1-3 days
  • Standard Delivery (平邮) - Slower but cheaper, usually 3-7 days
  • Same-day Delivery (当日达) - Available in some cities for nearby sellers

International Shipping

For international buyers, there are two main approaches:

Direct International Shipping

Some Taobao sellers offer international shipping, but this is relatively rare and may be expensive.

Forwarding Services

The most common method for international buyers. These services provide a Chinese warehouse address, consolidate your packages, and ship them internationally.

Popular Forwarding Services

Here are some well-known forwarding services used by international Taobao shoppers:

Superbuy

User-friendly interface with English support and good customer service.

Wegobuy

Similar to Superbuy with competitive shipping rates.

Basetao

Known for lower service fees and good QC photos.

Shipping options selection screen

Pro Tip

When using a forwarding service, consider consolidating multiple packages to save on international shipping costs. Most services offer package consolidation for a small fee.

Order Tracking

Tracking Your Orders

Taobao provides comprehensive order tracking. Here's how to track your purchases:

1

Access Orders

Go to "My Taobao" and tap on "My Orders" to see all your purchases.

2

Find Your Order

Orders are organized by status: Awaiting Payment, Awaiting Shipment, Awaiting Delivery, and Completed.

3

View Tracking Details

Tap on an order and select "Logistics Information" to see detailed tracking updates.

Understanding Order Status

Your order will go through several statuses:

  • Order Placed - Your order has been submitted but not yet paid
  • Paid - Payment has been received, waiting for seller to ship
  • Shipped - Seller has shipped the item
  • In Transit - Item is on its way to you
  • Delivered - Item has been delivered
  • Completed - Transaction is complete after confirmation of receipt

Taobao order tracking interface

Important Note

Taobao has an automatic confirmation of receipt after a certain period (usually 10-15 days). If you haven't received your item by then, make sure to extend the confirmation period.

Communicating with Sellers

Messaging System

Taobao has a built-in messaging system called Aliwangwang that allows you to chat with sellers. Here's how to use it:

1

Access Chat

On a product page or store page, tap the "Contact Seller" button to start a conversation.

2

Send Messages

Type your message in the chat box. You can also send images or use translation tools.

3

View Chat History

Access all your conversations by going to "My Taobao" and selecting "Messages".

Communication Tips

When messaging sellers who may not speak your language:

  • Use simple, clear language
  • Include product links or order numbers when asking questions
  • Use Taobao's built-in translation tool or copy messages to an external translator
  • Be patient - there may be time zone differences and language barriers

Taobao messaging interface

Pro Tip

Save common phrases in Chinese for frequently asked questions, such as "Do you ship internationally?" (您是否提供国际运输?) or "Is this item in stock?" (这个商品有库存吗?).

Ratings and Reviews

Understanding the Review System

Taobao's review system helps buyers make informed decisions and rewards good sellers. Here's how it works:

Rating System

Buyers can rate products on a scale of 1-5 stars for different aspects like product quality, seller service, and shipping speed.

Written Reviews

Buyers can leave detailed comments about their experience and upload photos of the received items.

Helpful Votes

Users can mark reviews as helpful, which increases their visibility to other shoppers.

Leaving a Review

After receiving and confirming your order, you'll be prompted to leave a review. Here's how:

1

Access Review Page

Go to "My Orders" and find the order marked "Awaiting Review" or tap the "Review" button on a completed order.

2

Rate the Product

Select a star rating for different aspects of your purchase experience.

3

Write Comments

Share your thoughts about the product, including pros and cons.

4

Add Photos (Optional)

Upload photos of the product to help other buyers see the actual item.

5

Submit Review

Tap "Submit" to publish your review.

Taobao review submission page

Pro Tip

When shopping, look for products with many reviews and photos from buyers. These "real-world" images often give you a better idea of what to expect than the seller's promotional photos.

Ready to Start Shopping?

Now that you understand how to use Taobao, you're ready to explore China's largest online marketplace. Remember to start with smaller purchases as you get familiar with the platform, and don't hesitate to contact sellers with questions.
